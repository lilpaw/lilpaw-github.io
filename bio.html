In 2011, Brandi was introduced to computer programming. For the first time in what seemed like forever, she felt truly challenged and it was wonderful! Over the next few years, she worked through instructional courses in Ruby and Python. Eventually, it became clear to her that she needed to change careers and become a software developer.

Brandi enjoys a lot in life, including target archery, photography, music, films, travel, and animals. She lives in the Bay Area with her boyfriend and their two adorable cats.
